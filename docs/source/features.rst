Features
========

The |sa| comprises two utilities for clubs and RORs to supporting meet sanctioning and officials development.

The system uses data exported from the |SNC|_ |RTR|_.  RTR exports must be after September 1, 2023 to contain all of the necessary data.

- Multi-Club Meet Sanctioning Reports for RORs/POAs
- Single Club Meet Sanctioning Report for COAs
- Co-Hosting Meet Sanctioning Report
- Officials Development Reports
- Mass e-mail Development Reports to Officials
